What is Flappy Leylek?

Flappy Leylek is a game with simple, yet incredibly enjoyable gameplay. Players try to survive for as long as possible by avoiding obstacles that appear on the screen. The main goal of the game is to achieve the highest score.

How to Play?

Playing Flappy Leylek is quite simple. Players make the stork jump by pressing the W key. Due to the effect of gravity, the stork continuously moves downwards, so players must jump at the correct moment. Finding the right timing is crucial to avoid the obstacles that appear on the screen. Players earn points for successfully passing each obstacle, and the difficulty of the game increases over time.
